ARTICLE 2 – PRINCIPLES OF PERSONAL-DATA PROCESSING

In accordance with Article 5 of the EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR), personal data are:

  • Processed lawfully, fairly and transparently;
  • Collected for explicit and legitimate purposes;
  • Adequate, relevant and limited to what is necessary;
  • Accurate and kept up to date;
  • Stored no longer than necessary; and
  • Protected by appropriate security measures.

Processing is lawful only if at least one of the following applies:

  • The user has given consent;
  • Processing is necessary for performance of a contract;
  • Processing is required by law; or
  • Processing is based on a legitimate interest that does not override the user’s fundamental rights.

ARTICLE 3 – DATA COLLECTED AND PROCESSED

3.1 – Data collected

The application collects no personally identifiable data (such as name, email address or location).

Only information strictly necessary to manage subscriptions is handled via the react-native-iap library.

3.2 – Collection methods

While using the application, the following non-identifying information may be processed locally:

  • Basic technical details (e.g. operating-system type) to ensure proper functionality;
  • In-app purchase data (subscription tier, purchase date, renewal status).

These data are used solely to enable subscription features. No data are stored on external servers and no advertising identifiers (IDFA, AAID) are used.

3.3 – Data hosting

The application has no backend server and stores no user data on remote hosting.

All necessary information is handled locally on the user’s device.

3.4 – Data sharing with third parties

No data are shared with third parties. The app relies on no external analytics or tracking providers.

3.5 – Cookies

The mobile application sets no cookies and performs no online tracking outside the app itself.
