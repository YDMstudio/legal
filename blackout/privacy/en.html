Privacy Policy – Blackout – Party Game

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Blackout – Party Game (hereinafter referred to as "the App") manages data and protects user privacy. The App is operated in accordance with French law by a sole proprietorship and is intended exclusively for adult users.

We are committed to ensuring transparency and compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR"). This Policy outlines the types of information that may be stored locally on your device, the purposes for which it is used, and the measures implemented to ensure your data remains private and secure.

This Policy applies solely to the App and its related features. It does not apply to third-party websites, services, or platforms, even if they are accessible through the App.

DATA MANAGED ON YOUR DEVICE

We may collect and process the following information about you:

Personal information including, for example, your usage of the Products and information collected by local tracking technologies as further described below that may identify your subscription status or app settings. Blackout – Party Game does not collect or process credit or debit card (“Payment Card”) information. Apple and Google collect Payment Card information with respect to in-app purchases made through the App. Such payment processors may provide us with limited information related to you, such as a unique purchase token, the subscription plan type (weekly, monthly, or yearly), the transaction date, and the expiration date.

Device information such as operating system version, device type, and system performance data may be used locally by the app to ensure proper functioning, but is not transmitted externally. No remote analytics or data tracking technologies are embedded in the App.

LEGAL BASIS FOR DATA PROCESSING

In accordance with Article 6 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the App does not collect, transmit, or process any personal data as defined under Article 4(1) of the Regulation. All features operate entirely on your device, and the technical data processed locally (such as subscription status or preferences) is not linked to any identifiable individual and remains strictly confined to your device.

As no personal data is collected or processed within the meaning of the GDPR, no specific legal basis for processing is required or applicable.

CONFIDENTIALITY AND SECURITY

The security of your personal information is important to us. However, Blackout – Party Game does not collect, transmit or store any personal information. All data related to your use of the App (such as subscription status or settings) is stored locally on your device.

We do not share or transmit any user data to third parties. No external server, analytics, or cloud service is used. If you have any questions about the security or integrity of data handled by the App, you may contact us at blackout.dev.app@gmail.com.

INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

No data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA). All information processed by the App remains stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or other jurisdictions.

DATA RETENTION

All data handled by the App remains stored locally on your device and is retained only for as long as the App remains installed. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all associated local data and preferences.

SYSTEM LOGS

The App does not access, collect, or transmit system crash logs or diagnostic data.

NEWSLETTERS OR OTHER ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATIONS

The App does not offer any newsletter, email subscription, or email-based promotional communications. We do not collect your email address and do not send marketing emails.

If you no longer wish to receive push notifications from the App, you may disable them at any time through your device’s system settings.

TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

The App does not use cookies, web beacons, advertising tags, tracking scripts, or any analytics SDKs. We do not collect or process advertising identifiers such as Apple’s IDFA or Google’s AAID, and we do not engage in behavioral profiling or targeted advertising.

No third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or Facebook Pixel are integrated in the App. We do not track user activity across other applications or websites.

All data related to gameplay and subscriptions is stored locally on your device and is not shared externally.

ACCESS TO AND DELETION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. However, since the App does not collect, process, or store any personal data, these rights are not applicable insofar as the App does not collect or process any personal data, as defined under the GDPR.

No user account is required to use the App, and no identifying information is transmitted or retained. All data remains local to your device.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding data privacy, you may contact us at blackout.dev.app@gmail.com.

Use of Local Data

The App processes only a limited set of technical information, stored locally on your device, in order to ensure proper functioning and personalize your experience.

This includes, for example, your subscription status and gameplay preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not used for advertising, analytics, or commercial purposes.

Use of Blackout – Party Game is strictly prohibited for individuals under the age of 18, or under the age of majority in their country of residence if higher.

The App is intended exclusively for adult users. By accessing or using the App, you confirm that you meet the required legal age. If you are underage, you are not authorized to use the App under any circumstances.
