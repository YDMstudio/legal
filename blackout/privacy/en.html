The technical data processed by the Application, particularly those related to subscription status, are necessary to provide access to paid features. This processing is based on the legal ground of contract performance, as set out in Article 6.1(b) of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Processing is limited to what is strictly necessary to verify a user’s eligibility for certain features and is not used for any commercial, advertising, or behavioral purposes.

The Application does not transmit any personal data to third parties, except for the technical information required to verify a subscription. This data is securely managed via the RevenueCat solution, which applies industry-standard security measures.

No personal information, email address, or payment details are collected, stored, or accessible by the publisher. Purchase transactions are handled exclusively by the App Store or Google Play platforms.
